Learn More

Tips to grow your business on Tradease.

Complete Your Profile

Contractors with complete profiles — photos, bio, pricing, and availability — receive up to 5x more booking requests. Fill in every section to rank higher in search results.

How Job Matching Works

When a customer searches for your trade in your area, Tradease ranks contractors by rating, profile completeness, and response rate. The higher your score, the more visible you are.

Response Rate Matters

Accept or decline bookings within 24 hours. Contractors with fast response times rank significantly higher in search results. Repeated declines affect your reliability score.

Building Reviews

After each completed job, customers are prompted to leave a review. The more five-star reviews you earn, the more bookings you'll receive. Deliver quality work and follow up professionally.

Upgrade to Pro

Pro contractors receive top search placement, a verified badge, lower platform fees, and access to analytics. It's the fastest way to grow your business on Tradease.
