Learn More

Everything you need to know about Tradease.

What is Tradease?

Tradease is New York's fastest-growing contractor booking platform. We connect homeowners with verified, local contractors — from plumbers and electricians to cleaners and landscapers — in seconds. No phone tag, no waiting. Just book and get it done.

How Booking Works

  • Search for the service you need or browse by trade category
  • Review contractor profiles, ratings, and real customer reviews
  • Choose a contractor and request a booking with your preferred date and time
  • The contractor confirms — you'll get notified instantly
  • After the job, leave a review to help the community

Are Contractors Verified?

Every contractor on Tradease goes through our approval process. Verified contractors display a green ✓ badge on their profile. We check licenses, insurance status, and review history before approving any contractor.

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your appointment. Cancellations within 48–24 hours incur a 25% fee. Within 12 hours: 50% fee. Same-day cancellations: 100% fee. This protects contractors' time and ensures reliability for everyone.

Want to Become a Contractor?

If you're a skilled tradesperson, sign up as a contractor and start receiving job requests today. It's free to join.
