Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Just Leads and Free?
Free lets you receive booking requests through Tradease but has limited visibility. Just Leads gives you a full featured profile that appears prominently in searches and gets recommended by our AI — no booking system involved.
Can Just Leads contractors still get reviews?
Yes. Customers who find you through Tradease can leave a review on your profile. Reviews build trust and help you rank higher in search results over time.
Can I upgrade from Just Leads to Pro later?
Absolutely. You can switch plans anytime from your contractor dashboard with no penalties. The price difference is prorated automatically.
What platform fee do Pro contractors pay?
Free plan: 15% per completed job. Pro plan: 10% per completed job. Just Leads: no fees — you don't process bookings through Tradease.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a contractor?
Search for the service you need, browse available contractors, select one, and choose an available time slot. Confirm your booking in one click — no phone calls needed.
Are all contractors background-checked?
Yes. Every contractor on Tradease goes through a comprehensive background check, license verification, and insurance review before they can accept bookings.
What happens if I need to cancel?
You can cancel a booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a small cancellation fee.
How does the platform fee work?
Tradease charges a small service fee on each completed job — 12% for Free plan contractors and just 5% for Pro subscribers. There are no hidden charges for customers.
How do I become a contractor on Tradease?
Click "Become a Contractor" in the nav, create a free account, submit your license information, pass the background check, and build your profile. The whole process takes under 30 minutes.
Is Tradease available in my city?
We're growing fast! Tradease currently serves major metro areas across the United States and is expanding to new cities every month. Enter your zip code on the homepage to check availability.
